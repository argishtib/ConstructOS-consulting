Our Services

Construction project controls and scheduling expertise

At ConstructOS, we specialize in construction project controls and scheduling. Our services help you maintain accurate schedules, control costs, analyze delays, and generate comprehensive reports to support your construction projects.

Project Controls & Scheduling Services

📅

Schedule Builds

Professional construction schedule development from project initiation through completion. We create comprehensive, logic-driven schedules that accurately reflect project scope, sequence, and dependencies.

🔄

Schedule Updates

Regular schedule updates to reflect actual progress, changes, and current project status. We maintain schedule integrity while incorporating progress data and change orders.

💰

Schedule & Cost Updates

Integrated schedule and cost updates that link time and budget performance. We provide comprehensive updates that show both schedule progress and cost performance in a unified view.

🔍

Forensic Scheduling

Expert forensic scheduling analysis for delay claims and disputes. We provide objective analysis to identify causes of delays and their impacts on project completion.

⏱️

Time Impact Analysis

Detailed time impact analysis (TIA) to quantify the effect of changes, delays, and disruptions on project schedules. Our analysis supports claims preparation and dispute resolution.

🪟

Window Analysis

Window analysis to identify and quantify delays within specific time periods. This methodical approach helps isolate and analyze delays for more accurate claims assessment.

📊

Reporting

Comprehensive reporting services including progress reports, variance analysis, critical path updates, and executive summaries. We provide clear, actionable reports tailored to your audience.

📈

Forensic Analysis

In-depth forensic schedule analysis including critical path identification, float analysis, time impact analysis, and what-if scenario planning to support claims and dispute resolution.
