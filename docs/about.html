XER2XLSXGANTT

In addition to our consulting services, we operate XER2XLSXGANTT, a technology company focused on converting XER files (Primavera P6 format) to XLSX (Excel) format. This innovative solution makes Primavera schedules more accessible and easier to work with in Excel, enabling better collaboration and analysis.

XER2XLSXGANTT helps construction professionals who need to work with Primavera P6 schedules but prefer the flexibility and familiarity of Excel. Our conversion technology preserves schedule data integrity while providing the convenience of Excel's powerful analysis tools.
