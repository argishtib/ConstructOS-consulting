About ConstructOS

Consulting and technology solutions for construction projects

Our Story

ConstructOS specializes in construction project controls and scheduling, providing expert services to help construction companies manage their projects effectively. We understand that accurate scheduling, cost control, and forensic analysis are critical to project success.

Our team brings extensive experience in construction project controls, scheduling methodologies, and forensic scheduling analysis. We work with clients to build comprehensive schedules, maintain accurate cost and schedule updates, and provide expert analysis for claims and disputes.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional construction project controls and scheduling services that help our clients deliver projects on time and within budget. We combine deep scheduling expertise with innovative technology solutions to support better project outcomes.

Two Divisions

ConstructOS operates through two distinct divisions:

Consulting Division

Our Consulting division provides professional scheduling services, schedule builds and updates, forensic analysis, and project controls expertise. This is where we work directly with clients to build schedules, maintain updates, and support claims and dispute resolution.

Technology Division

Our Technology division develops innovative solutions for construction projects. This includes XER2XLSXGANTT (coming soon), which converts XER files (Primavera P6 format) to XLSX (Excel) format, making Primavera schedules more accessible and easier to work with in Excel.

XER2XLSXGANTT (coming soon) helps construction professionals who need to work with Primavera P6 schedules but prefer the flexibility and familiarity of Excel.

Service Areas
